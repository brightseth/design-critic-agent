Design Principles

The core philosophies guiding this critique

Simplicity First
Steve Jobs: "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." Remove until it breaks, then add back only what's essential.
Laws of Simplicity
John Maeda: Reduce, organize, time, learn, differences, context, emotion, trust, failure, and the one.
Belong Anywhere
Airbnb: Design for trust, community, and human connection. Every pixel should make people feel they belong.
Capture & Share
Instagram: Make the complex simple. Focus on the content, minimize the chrome. Speed and delight matter.
Inevitable Design
Jony Ive: "It should feel inevitable, not designed." The best design disappears and feels completely natural.
Emotional Resonance
Great design creates an emotional connection. It should delight, surprise, and make people feel something.
